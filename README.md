# GAMEJAM-ESMA2026
Project of MARCEL CAILLOT Evan, OMER VERNY Luna, LARGERON Thibault and GERY Valentin for the 72h ESMA Bordeaux 2026 game jam on the theme : "fragile".

In Bonne Nuit Pierrot, explore the nightmare of Pierrot, a young child, and try to survive until the end of the night. Your only light is a candle which's spark is slowly decaying. You must explore, find new candles, and avoid what's roaming in the corridors.

=======
CONTROLS
=======
WASD / ZQSD - move
E / left click - pick up candle

If the clock reaches 00:00, you win!
